Catheters and endotracheal tubes are inserted into patient's lungs to help them breathe. They are connected to respirators that deliver oxygen to the lungs. If tubes are improperly positioned, the effects are serious. It can cause injury to the throat, collapse the lung, and death.

Detecting if tubes are properly placed or not is a difficult task. An experienced doctor must analyze chest X-rays carefully, and their judgement takes time and may be inaccurate. With COVID-19, there is massive demand for tubes and strained medical support, which makes monitoring correct placement even more difficult.

We created a deep learning AI to automatically recognize if a tube is properly placed. This allows for accurate and quick results, potentially saving lives.
